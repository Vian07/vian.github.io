About My Cliques

Our Cliques has 11 people. This Cliques was made at our school in SMAK 6 Penabur. We made this cliques because we have a alot in common. Even though we are devided into 4 different classes, we are still friend. In our cliques there are those who come from SOCIAL classes and those who come from SCIENCE classes. Me and 9 of my friend are from SOCIAL classes, and the other 2 of my friend are from SCIENCE classes. We met each other when we were in High School. We have alot of memory together. We makes jokes together, and when some one of my friend tell some not funny jokes, we all quite and said that the jokes isn't funny. The Humoris Cliques name is come from my idea because all of my friend always laugh everyday and always make some jokes together or tell some funny story that happen in the school.

Story

We went there take the train, and at the time not all of our cliques member went to Bandung because some of our friend already have vacation with their family. We have alot of fun together. When we arrived, in the first day we only check in the hotel room and we take a rest at the hotel room. In the second day we want to play airsoft so in that day we booked the airsoft place, after we arrived at airsoft place, we got to listen some rules if we want to play airsoft because it can be dangerous if we didn't listen the briefing. After we listen some rules, we start playing. After we play airsoft, on that night we go again to play gokart. we spend alot of money in that place because we have so much fun. in the third day, we are going to packing our stuff because at that night we must go back to Jakarta. At the train we all take a rest and some of our friend go to sleep.
